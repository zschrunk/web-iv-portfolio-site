Respect The R–Word

Social Activism Campaign

Respect the r–word is a campaign that has gotten a lot of attention lately. They wanted a campaign that would bring more attention to what they are doing with a cost friendly budget, since they are a non–profit organization. Respect the r–word is a campaign that whats to stop the usage of the word “retard” to help prevent bulling. They wanted a campaign that was going to catch the attention of the viewer and draw them in. Their target audience was the 15 to 20 year olds who used the word. They are the ones that are out with their friends more, so that allowed the play with the environment.
