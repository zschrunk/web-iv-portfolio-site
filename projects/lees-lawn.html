Project

Lee's Lawn

Web Site with Process

Lee’s Lawn is a lawn care service that has been around for 25 years. It was started when Lee was a younger kid and he mowed lawns for extra money as a kid. He grew to love it and made his own company out of it and stuck with it. Lee came to the conclusion that it is time for a website to help spread his company. He was wanting a website that was easy to use and allowed his customers to get a free quote so they have an estimate of how much it would cost. Those were his big things that he wanted in the site. Lee is wanting to target the working class families that have disposable income and don’t have time to take care of their own lawns. His secondary audience is companies that need someone to take care of their grounds around the business itself.
