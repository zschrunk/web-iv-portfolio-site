Project

Romanée–Conti

Package Design

Romanee–Conti is an older wine that has been around for generations. They are a part of the Burgundy wine that is from Eastern France. Romanee–Conti was first made in 1631 when the vineyard was passed by marriage. Romanee–Conti wanted to target a higher class of people from the ages of 55 to 70. These people have disposable income and like to open a top of the line bottle of wine to celebrate when needed.
